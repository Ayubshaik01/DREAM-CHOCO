Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer free shipping on orders above 500 INR across India. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, and express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee.

Can I customize my chocolate order?

Yes! We offer customization for bulk orders and special occasions. Please reach out to us with your requirements, and we’ll create something special for you.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship only within India. We’re working on expanding internationally, so stay tuned!

What is your return policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you’re not happy with your order, contact us within 7 days for a refund or replacement.
